XUGS Infopad is an application that makes it easy for students, parents, teachers and other members of Xavier University Grade School to be informed and updated about our school.

XUGS Infopad is a free application. You can contact the author for help regarding making your own school application.

This application started as a project of the TechSurfers Club (Computer Club) SY 2013-2014 of XUGS. Since it was unfinished, the author continued its development and testing. This application is still in beta mode. Thus this application still needs hardware testing and feedback from users.

Features:

  • Splash Screen opening the application
  • Home shows the latest news
  • Schedules for reference
  • Handbook has school's information
  • Contact contains phone numbers
