XUGS Hymns

ALMA MATER SONG

Xavier, Alma Mater,
You have taught us to pay the price
To be men for God and country
To prepare for sacrifice
May we learn to love, may we learn to serve
And as Christ may we always give,
Our hands in help, our hearts in love
And our lives that other men might live

Xavier, Alma Mater
You have taught us to worship with pride
Our Savior's life and sufferings
And the cross on which He died
Not to fear the nails, not to heed the wounds
But to love throught the thorns and pains
Keep our faith alive in the face of death
That the world may see our Savior's reign

ON CRUSADERS

On Crusaders, blue and white
With ready swords and shinning lance
Bugles call us to the fight
Ateneans we advance

(Chorus)
Over plains and mountain trails
Until we face the bitter foe
And we will ride like fighting men
With our banners waving high
And our soldier's battle cry
Charge on Crusaders onward we'll win today!

On Crusaders of the Queen
Defend the honor of her name
And wherever we are seen
We will fight to uphold her fame

(Repeat Chorus)

LOYALTY SONG

We sing to thee our loyalty
O Alma Mater Xavier U
We give our hearts to you today
And promise to be true

We will keep your memories in our hearts
All the days we loved so well
O Hail dear Alma Mater
O Hail dear Xavier U

(Repeat second stanza)
