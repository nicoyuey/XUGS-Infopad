Honors

Deserving students are given due recognition at the end of the quarter and at the end of the school year.

First Honors

  • a Grade Point Average (GPA) of 95% or higher
  • no grade lower than 90% in all the subjects
  • no grade lower than Satisfactory or Good in Penmanship, Conduct, Effort, Homeroom, Prefect's Grade and Co-curricular Activities.

Second Honors

  • a Grade Point Average (GPA) of 92% or higher
  • no grade lower than 88% in all the subjects
  • no grade lower than Satisfactory or Good in Penmanship, Conduct, Effort, Homeroom, Prefect's Grade and Co-curricular Activities.

Honorable Mention

  • a Grade Point Average (GPA) of 90% or higher
  • no grade lower than 87% in all the subjects
  • no grade lower than Satisfactory or Good in Penmanship, Conduct, Effort, Homeroom, Prefect's Grade and Co-curricular Activities.
