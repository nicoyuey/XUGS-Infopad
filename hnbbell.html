Bell System

1 Long Bell

Signals the pupils to move inside their classrooms and prepare themselves for the school activities five (5) minutes before the start of the weekly assembly or first period class.

Silence Bell

Signals the readiness of each pupil for the opening prayer - by standing quietly beside one's chair.

1 Short Bell

Signals the saying of the opening prayer and the start of the class session. The Class President will lead the Opening Prayer if the teacher is still not around.
