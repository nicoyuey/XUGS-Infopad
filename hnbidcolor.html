Identification Card

Here are some of the rules regarding your school id:

1. Wear your ID at all times. Make sure that you do not lose your ID.

2. Inform the Prefect of Students right away if your ID is lost, and obtain a new ID.

3. Non-compliance of this policy will be dealt with accordingly.


Here are the School ID Color and their meaning:

  • BLUE

    Stay in for lunch,
    to be fetched in the afternoon

  • ORANGE

    Stay out for lunch,
    to be fetched in the afternoon

  • YELLOW

    Stay out for lunch,
    can go home alone

  • RED

    To be fetched by parents
    in the morning and afternoon
